You are a senior product designer, brand art director, frontend engineer, and conversion strategist.

Build a unique premium one-page Arabic RTL website for a B2B digital transformation agency/consultant. The website must not look like an AI-generated template. It should feel inspired by the design quality of Apple, Anthropic, Google, and Samsung, but do not copy any of their layouts, branding, components, typography, or exact visual language.

Project context:

The service includes business diagnosis, process mapping, automation, CRM setup, lead qualification, follow-up systems, dashboards, AI assistants, and implementation. The founder does not yet have case studies, so the main conversion offer is a free custom diagnosis before selling.

UI and UX rules:
- RTL must be correct.
- Buttons at least 44px high.
- Body text minimum 16px on mobile.
- Strong contrast.
- Inputs must have visible labels.
- Mobile layout must be excellent at 375px.
- No content overlap.
- No horizontal scroll.
- Images must have reserved aspect ratios to avoid layout shift.
- Use responsive image optimization.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
- Do not animate width, height, top, or left. Animate only transform and opacity.
- Use subtle scroll reveal with IntersectionObserver or Motion.
- Optional hero parallax must be very small and disabled on reduced motion.
- CTA hover may move a small inner arrow/icon slightly, but avoid gimmicks.

Motion:
Use restrained motion:
- Hero visual fades in and moves up slightly.
- Sections reveal on scroll with opacity and translateY.
- A thin emerald path can animate through the modules section.
- Form fields can have refined focus transitions.
No excessive animation.
No scroll hijacking unless perfectly implemented.
